Frequently Asked Question

Here are some of our FAQS. If you have any other questions you'd like answered, please feel free to email us.

How long does it typically take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary depending on factors such as project scope, complexity, and client feedback. I aim to deliver high-quality work efficiently and within mutually agreed-upon deadlines.

What is your availability for new projects?

My availability varies, but I'm committed to delivering excellent results in a timely manner. Please reach out to discuss your project timeline.

Do you require a deposit or advance payment?

Yes, I typically require a 50% deposit upfront to secure your spot in my schedule and commence work on your project.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the final deliverables?

Client satisfaction is my priority, and I strive to deliver high-quality work that meets your expectations. If you're not satisfied with the final deliverables, we'll work together to address any concerns and make necessary revisions until you're happy with the result.

What happens if the project scope changes during the process?

If there are changes to the project scope, we'll discuss and adjust accordingly. Any significant changes may result in revisions to the timeline and cost, which will be communicated and agreed upon transparently.
